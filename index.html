Arithmetic and Topology

Southern University of Science and Technology

About

This series of events is hosted by the Department of Mathematics and the Shenzhen International Center for Mathematics at SUSTech. We intend to bring experts in both Number Theory and Topology to share recent advances in these related fields, and to foster interdisciplinary communication and collaboration.
